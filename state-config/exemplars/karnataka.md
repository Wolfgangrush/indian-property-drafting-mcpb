# State Exemplar — Karnataka (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Karnataka Stamp Act 1957 (with subsequent State amendments)

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (blood-relative) | 28 | ₹1,000 - ₹5,000 nominal (concessional) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 28 | Same as Sale (typically 5%) |
| Exchange Deed | 23 | Higher of two at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 51A | ₹500 - ₹1,000 |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 51A | Same as Sale (typically 5%) |
| Private Trust Deed | 47 | Linked to corpus value |
| Wakf Deed | (linked to religious-charitable provisions) | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5 | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 41 (Section 7 ISA framework adopted) | Concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 41-A | ₹1,000 - ₹5,000 (concessional) |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 34 | Ad valorem on secured amount |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Ad valorem at lower rate |

## Guidance Value (Karnataka's Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Sub-Registrar Office; Karnataka State Stamp Department
- **Portal:** https://kaverionline.karnataka.gov.in (Kaveri-2 portal)
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Guidance Value is the ad valorem basis

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Bengaluru — Sub-Registrar circles 1 to 25+
- Mysuru, Hubli-Dharwad, Mangaluru, Belagavi — district headquarters circles
- Tehsil-level Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **Agricultural land:** RTC (Record of Rights, Tenancy and Crop Inspection) under Karnataka Land Revenue Act 1964
- **Urban property:** Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) records / Municipal records
- **Flats in CHS:** Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act 1959

## Karnataka-specific overlays
- **Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act 1972** — for flats
- **Karnataka Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2017** — RERA
- **Karnataka Land Reforms Act 1961** — restrictions on agricultural-land transfer (Sections 79A, 79B — recently amended in 2020 to remove income-cap restrictions for non-agriculturists)

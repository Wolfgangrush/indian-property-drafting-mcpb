# State Exemplar — Maharashtra (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Maharashtra Stamp Act 1958 (with subsequent State amendments)
- **Schedule I** — Article-wise stamp duty for property instruments

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification — Maharashtra Government's latest Stamp Act notification or e-Stamp portal)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (to blood-relative — spouse, lineal ascendant/descendant, sibling) | 34 | ₹200 / 1% / 2% of market value (concessional — verify current) |
| Gift Deed (to non-blood-relative) | 34 | Same as Sale (typically 5%) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher of the two properties at Sale rate (typically 5%); each leg separately stamped |
| Release Deed (blood-relative releasor/releasee) | 55 | ₹200 |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale (typically 5%) |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to trust corpus value |
| Wakf Deed (religious / charitable) | 65 | Concessional (verify); typically nominal |
| Wakf-alal-Aulad | 65 | Linked to corpus value |
| Easement Deed | 5(a) | Nominal (Agreement rate, typically ₹100-₹500) |
| Partition Deed (Section 7 ISA highest-share aggregate) | 46 | Concessional among coparceners (verify); typically lower than separate Sale |
| Family Settlement Deed (Kale-framework) | 58 | ₹200 - ₹500 (significantly concessional) |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount (typically 0.5% - 5% depending on Mortgagee type) |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Ad valorem on secured amount at LOWER rate than Article 40 |
| Title Investigation Report | N/A | No stamp (legal opinion document) |

## Ready Reckoner / Annual Statement of Rates
- **Authority:** Inspector General of Registration (IGR), Maharashtra
- **Portal:** https://igrmaharashtra.gov.in
- **Year of Reference:** Latest IGR notification (annual update)
- **Application:** Higher of (a) consideration in the deed and (b) Ready Reckoner value is the basis for ad valorem stamp duty

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **For agricultural land:** 7/12 Extract under Maharashtra Land Revenue Code 1966 (Sections 148-152)
- **For urban property:** Municipal Property Records
- **For flats in CHS:** Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act 1960 — Society Share Certificate + Form No. 14 / Form No. 17 for transfer

## Suraj Lamp discipline notification status
- Maharashtra has strictly enforced Suraj Lamp; GPA-Sale rejected at Sub-Registrar; further reinforced by State Stamp Department circulars

## Section 47A reference framework
- Sub-Registrar refers under-valuation to the Collector under Section 47A Indian Stamp Act 1899 + Section 32A Registration Act 1908; Collector determines true market value

## Maharashtra-specific overlays
- **Maharashtra Apartment Ownership Act 1970** — for flats; declaration of apartment ownership requires registration
- **Maharashtra Ownership Flats Act 1963 (MOFA)** — buyer protection (largely subsumed by RERA 2016 for new projects)
- **Maharashtra Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2017** — under RERA 2016
- **Maharashtra Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act 1948** — for agricultural-land sales, Section 63 restrictions on non-agriculturist purchasers

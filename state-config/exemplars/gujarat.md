# State Exemplar — Gujarat (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Bombay Stamp Act 1958 as adapted by Gujarat (Gujarat Stamp Act), with subsequent Gujarat amendments

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (blood-relative) | 34 | ₹200 - 3.5% (concessional) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 34 | Same as Sale (typically 4.9%) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher leg at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 55 | ₹200 - ₹500 nominal |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to corpus |
| Wakf Deed | 65 | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5 | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 46 (Section 7 ISA) | Concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 58 | Nominal (₹500 - ₹1,000) |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Lower rate |

## Jantri Rate (Gujarat Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Inspector General of Registration (IGR), Gujarat
- **Portal:** https://garvi.gujarat.gov.in
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Jantri Rate is the ad valorem basis

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Ahmedabad, Vadodara, Surat, Rajkot, Gandhinagar — district headquarters + tehsil-level Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **Agricultural land:** 7/12 Extract under Gujarat Land Revenue Code 1879
- **Urban property:** Municipal records / Development Authority records (AUDA, VUDA, SUDA, etc.)
- **Flats in CHS:** Gujarat Co-operative Societies Act 1961

## Gujarat-specific overlays
- **Gujarat Ownership Flats Act 1973** — for flats (largely subsumed by RERA for new projects)
- **Gujarat Apartment Ownership Act 2014**
- **Gujarat Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2017** — RERA
- **Gujarat Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act 1948** — Section 63 restrictions on non-agriculturist purchasers

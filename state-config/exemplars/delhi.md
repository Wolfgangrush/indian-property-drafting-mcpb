# State Exemplar — Delhi (NCT) (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Indian Stamp Act 1899 as in force in Delhi (with Delhi-specific amendments)

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (to blood-relative — spouse, lineal asc/desc, sibling) | 34 | 4% of market value (concessional; verify Delhi-specific amendment) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 34 | 6% of market value (same as Sale) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher leg at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 55 | ₹100 - ₹500 nominal |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to corpus |
| Wakf Deed | 65 | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5 | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 45 (Section 7 ISA) | Concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 58 | Nominal (₹500 - ₹1,000 typical) |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount (0.5% - 4%) |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Lower rate |

## Circle Rate (Delhi Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Government of NCT of Delhi — Department of Revenue
- **Portal:** https://doris.delhigovt.nic.in
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Circle Rate is the ad valorem basis
- **Categories:** Delhi properties are categorised A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H — different rates per category

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Central Delhi, New Delhi, North Delhi, South Delhi, East Delhi, West Delhi, North-East, South-East, North-West, South-West, Shahdara — separate Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **DDA flats:** Delhi Development Authority — DDA Allotment / Conveyance records
- **MCD / NDMC records:** for municipal property
- **Flats in CHS:** Delhi Co-operative Societies Act 2003
- **Patwari records:** for residual agricultural / urban-villages property

## Delhi-specific overlays
- **Delhi Apartment Ownership Act 1986** — for flats
- **Delhi RERA framework** — under RERA 2016
- **Delhi Lands (Restrictions on Transfer) Act 1972** — restrictions on transfer of certain government-allotted lands
- **DDA flats** — special conveyancing discipline; Power of Attorney + Sale via DDA's online portal for re-sale; Suraj Lamp applies strictly post-2012

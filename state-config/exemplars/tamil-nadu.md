# State Exemplar — Tamil Nadu (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Indian Stamp Act 1899 as adapted by Tamil Nadu, with subsequent Tamil Nadu amendments

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (blood-relative) | 34 | 1% of market value (concessional) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 34 | Same as Sale (typically 7%) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher leg at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 55 | 1% (concessional) |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to corpus value |
| Wakf Deed (religious / charitable) | 65 | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5(a) | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 46 (Section 7 ISA highest-share-aggregate) | 1% of market value, concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 58 | 1% of market value |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount (0.5% - 4%) |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Lower rate |

## Guideline Value (Tamil Nadu Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Inspector General of Registration (IGR), Tamil Nadu
- **Portal:** https://tnreginet.gov.in
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Guideline Value is the ad valorem basis

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Chennai — Sub-Registrar circles
- District headquarters and tehsil-level Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **Agricultural land:** Patta + Chitta + UDR (Updated Dynamic Record) under Tamil Nadu Patta Pass Book Act 1983
- **Urban property:** Municipal records / Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority (CMDA) records
- **Flats in CHS:** Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act 1983

## Tamil Nadu-specific overlays
- **Tamil Nadu Apartment Ownership Act 1994** — for flats
- **Tamil Nadu Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2017** — RERA
- **Tamil Nadu Land Reforms (Fixation of Ceiling on Land) Act 1961** — ceiling restrictions

# State Exemplar — Uttar Pradesh (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Indian Stamp Act 1899 as in force in Uttar Pradesh, with UP-specific amendments

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification — UP IGR portal)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (blood-relative) | 34 | ₹5,000 maximum (significantly concessional in UP for family) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 34 | Same as Sale (typically 7%) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher leg at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 55 | Nominal (₹100 - ₹500) |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to corpus value |
| Wakf Deed | 65 | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5 | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 45 (Section 7 ISA) | Concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 58 | Nominal |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Lower rate |

## Circle Rate (UP Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Inspector General of Stamps and Revenue, UP
- **Portal:** https://igrsup.gov.in
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Circle Rate is the ad valorem basis

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Lucknow, Kanpur, Allahabad (Prayagraj), Varanasi, Agra, Meerut, Ghaziabad, Noida, Gorakhpur — district headquarters + tehsil-level Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **Agricultural land:** Khatauni / Khasra under UP Land Revenue Act 1901 + UP Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act 1950
- **Urban property:** Nagar Nigam records / Development Authority records (LDA, KDA, GDA, NOIDA, etc.)
- **Flats in CHS:** UP Co-operative Societies Act 1965

## UP-specific overlays
- **UP Apartment (Promotion of Construction, Ownership and Maintenance) Act 2010** — for flats
- **Uttar Pradesh Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2016** — RERA
- **UP Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Act 1950** — Section 154 ceiling on agricultural-land transfer; non-resident purchaser restrictions
- **UP Urban Planning and Development Act 1973** — restrictions on conversion of agricultural to non-agricultural use

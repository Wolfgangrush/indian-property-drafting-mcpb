# State Exemplar — Telangana (Property)

## Applicable Stamp Act
- **Act:** Indian Stamp Act 1899 as in force in Telangana (inherited from Andhra Pradesh framework with Telangana-specific amendments post-bifurcation in 2014)

## Article-wise stamp duty (verify current notification)

| Instrument | Article | Rate (typical) |
|---|---|---|
| Gift Deed (blood-relative) | 34 | ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 nominal (concessional) |
| Gift Deed (non-relative) | 34 | Same as Sale (typically 5%) |
| Exchange Deed | 32 | Higher leg at Sale rate; dual stamp |
| Release Deed (blood-relative) | 55 | Nominal |
| Release Deed (non-relative) | 55 | Same as Sale |
| Private Trust Deed | 64 | Linked to corpus value |
| Wakf Deed | 65 | Concessional |
| Easement Deed | 5 | Nominal |
| Partition Deed | 46 (Section 7 ISA) | Concessional among coparceners |
| Family Settlement Deed | 58 | Nominal |
| Mortgage Deed (registered) | 40 | Ad valorem on secured amount |
| Equitable Mortgage Memorandum | 6 | Lower rate |

## Market Value (Telangana Ready Reckoner equivalent)
- **Authority:** Telangana Department of Registration and Stamps
- **Portal:** https://registration.telangana.gov.in
- **Application:** Higher of consideration and Market Value is the ad valorem basis

## Sub-Registrar circles
- Hyderabad — Sub-Registrar circles 1 to 24+
- Secunderabad — separate circles
- District headquarters + tehsil-level Sub-Registrar offices

## Mutation / Revenue Records
- **Agricultural land:** Pahani / 1B Adangal under Telangana Land Revenue framework
- **Urban property:** Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC) records
- **Flats in CHS:** Telangana Co-operative Societies Act 1964

## Telangana-specific overlays
- **Telangana Apartments (Promotion of Construction and Ownership) Act 1987** (inherited from AP)
- **Telangana Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Rules 2017** — RERA
- **Telangana Rights in Land and Pattadar Pass Books Act 1971** — Pattadar Pass Book system
- **Telangana Land Reforms (Ceiling on Agricultural Holdings) Act 1973** — ceiling restrictions
- **Dharani portal** — Telangana's integrated land-records and registration portal (mandatory for all property registrations)
